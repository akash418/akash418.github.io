Akash Kumar Gautam

I am a research assistant at MIDAS Labs, IIIT-Delhi. Along with having experience in the software industry, I have been working under the guidance of Dr Rajiv Ratn Shah and Dr Debanjan Mahata. I have also collaborated with Dr Rakesh Gosangi and Dr Raghava Mutharaju on numerous projects. I completed my undergraduation from IIIT-Delhi (2015-2019) in Computer Science and Engineering. My research interests broadly consist of topics related to Natural Language Processing, Computational Social Science, Information Retrieval, and Knowledge Graphs. Please feel free to reach out for potential collaboration opportunities.
